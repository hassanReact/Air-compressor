REFRIGERATED DRYER

The refrigerated dryer mainly uses the refrigerant and compressed air for heat exchange, and reduces the compressed air temperature to the dew point temperature in the range of 2 ~ 10℃, so that the water content in the compressed air tends to the state of super saturation, so as to remove the water in the compressed air to ensure the stability of production. harmful substances pollution.

A Refrigerated Dryer is essential for maintaining the quality and efficiency of compressed air systems in various industrial applications. By cooling the compressed air to a temperature below its dew point, these dryers effectively remove moisture, preventing corrosion, freeze-ups, and equipment damage.
